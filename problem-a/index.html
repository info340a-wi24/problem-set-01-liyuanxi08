Pingu

Pingu is the main character from the Swiss-British stop-motion clay animated children's television series titled Pingu. He is a fun little penguin who loves to play and get into adventures. He lives in a snowy place with his family and friends. Pingu talks in a funny way that isn't real words, which makes him really special. Everyone likes Pingu because he's cute and his stories are about friendship and family.

  • Pingu often finds himself in sticky situations, but his ingenuity always shines through.
  • Pingu always embarks on new adventures, displaying a sense of curiosity and bravery.
  • Pingu has an incredible range of emotions despite having such simple features.
